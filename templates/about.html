Our History

Elite Social Club was established in 2015 by a group of cricket enthusiasts who shared a common dream - to create a platform where talent meets opportunity. Starting with just 11 players and a small practice ground, we have grown into a formidable force in the district circuit.

Today, we are a community that nurtures young talent, promotes sportsmanship, and competes at the highest levels.
